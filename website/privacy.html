Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

Our Promise

Seahorse Centaur Studios makes kids’ games with no ads. We aim to collect as little information as possible.

Information We Collect

We may collect limited information depending on how you use our website and games:

  • Website contact email: If you email us, we receive your email address and the content of your message.
  • App store information: Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple, Google, or Amazon. We do not receive your payment card details.
  • Crash/diagnostic data (optional): If enabled in an app, we may receive anonymized crash logs to improve stability.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not show third-party ads.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to messages you send us.
  • To improve our games (for example, fixing bugs).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Third-Party Services

Our games may be distributed through third-party platforms (Apple App Store, Google Play, Amazon Appstore). These platforms may collect information under their own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

Our games are designed for kids and families. We strive to respect children’s privacy and comply with applicable laws (such as COPPA in the United States). If you believe we have collected personal information from a child in error, please contact us and we will work to delete it.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at hello@seahorsecentaur.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above when changes are made.
